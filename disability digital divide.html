Disability Digital Divide

Disability Gap in Mobile Internet Usage in India

Only 27% of disabled persons use Mobile internet compared to 40% of non-disabled
persons. Mobiles play an important role in helping persons with disabilities to live
independent lives and access basic services. Smartphones can put multiple assistive
technologies into one device, enabling more independent living for persons with disabilities
but according to research, people with disabilities are less likely than non-disabled
persons to own a mobile, especially a smartphone, and to be aware of or use
mobile internet. Key barriers to using mobile for persons with disabilities include the
disability itself, awareness of accessibility features, affordability and levels of
literacy and skills.
