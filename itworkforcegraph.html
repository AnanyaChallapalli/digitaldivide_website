Discrimination in IT Sector

“Since Hindu upper castes constitute almost 67% of engineering and technology graduates,
it would not be surprising to find that upwards of 70% of the IT workforce are upper caste,”
wrote Carol Upadhya in her 2007 paper “Employment, Exclusion and ‘Merit’ in the Indian
IT Industry." In the year 2009, a study in Chennai said that the lowest job application callbacks
in the private sector are received by dalit women. In the year 2020, a group of 30 female
Indian engineers who are members of the Dalit caste and work for Google, Apple, Microsoft,
Cisco and other tech companies say they have faced caste bias inside the U.S. tech sector.

As we can see, there is a caste based bias in the tech industry making it difficult for people
of lower castes to work in the tech sector.
