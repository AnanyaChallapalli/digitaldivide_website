Digital Divide in Education

Children with Access to Online Education during the Pandemic

Only 20 per cent of school-age children in India had access to remote education during
the pandemic, of whom only half participated in live online lessons. According to a
recent Oxfam research, up to 80% of Indian students were unable to access online
learning during the lockdown, and many may not return to their classes after the epidemic
is over.The pandemic led to an increased risk of a large number of girls dropping out
of their schools permanently; one of the estimates suggested that around 11 million girls
would not return to school when the crisis is over.In fact, 38 per cent of households
said at least one child had dropped out of school due to Covid-19.
